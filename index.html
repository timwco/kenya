More About Our Trip

This summer, Tim and I are going to Kenya with Canopy Life! It’ll be our first time out of the country as a couple, and we’re filled with anticipation for the opportunity to serve and grow together.

Since 2014, I have been a part of Canopy Life’s home team, spending several hours a month writing about the school, learning about Kenyan education, and telling the stories of our pint-sized students. Tim and I sponsor a student at the school and have seen her world through a 13-inch screen via Skype. It’s a dream of ours to meet Rose and her classmates face-to-face, exchange high fives and smiles, and be part of their classroom environment.

Read more of our story in my blog post We’re Going to Kenya!

Tim and I would like to invite you to consider supporting our journey to Kenya by signing up for email updates, giving financially (our goal is to raise $6000), and telling the Canopy Life story to others. The school has been near-and-dear to our hearts since before its doors even opened, and it’s a privilege for us to get to experience it first-hand.

Thank you so much for your support and being a part of helping us make this amazing dream a reality.
